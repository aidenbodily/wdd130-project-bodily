What I Use

This is a list of all the gear and software I use for my projects

I have mainly recently been using the Ordro HDV-V88, which is an old cheap Chinese 1080p camcorder that has barely any surviving evidence on the internet. I’m hoping to acquire an actual VHS camera sometime soon to help with the nostalgic look but this is the best I’ve got at the moment.

I use a Pentax Espoo 928, which is a simple point and shoot with a zoom that was released by Pentax in 1994, I bought it on eBay for around $35 and typically use Kodak Gold 200, or Kodak Portra 400/800 to shoot.

I use Adobe Premiere Pro and occasionally Adobe After Effects.

I don’t typically edit my photos, but if I do I use Adobe Photoshop or Adobe Lightroom.

I use Adobe Illustrator and occasionally Adobe Photoshop.
